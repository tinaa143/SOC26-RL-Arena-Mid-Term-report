
## How to Run

Press "SPACE" to start the game.

## Controls

- Red: W A S D
- Blue: Arrow Keys
- SPACE: Start or restart

## How to Play

- Red and Blue each start with a small territory.
- Move outside your territory to make a trail.
- Come back to your own territory to capture the area inside.
- If you touch your own trail, you die.
- If you touch Red or Blue's trail, that color dies.
- Moving into the other color's territory is blocked.
- Going outside the grid is blocked.
- Red or Blue goes outside their own territory and leaves a trail.
- When that color comes back into their own territory, the trail and border make a closed shape.
- The empty area inside that shape becomes that color's new territory.
